Scones

Description

These scones are sure to be the highlight at every event. Don't wait too long before serving these to make sure that they stay soft and moist. Best served with a side of Devonshire Cream and/or powdered sugar.

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and sugar together.
  2. Add the baking powder and salt to the mixture.
  3. Melt the butter and add to the dry ingredients.
  4. Add the heavy whipping cream.
  5. Beat the eggs and then add to the mixture.
  6. Add the vanilla and completely mix.
  7. Pour mixed ingredients onto the counter and form into small cakes
  8. Place cakes on pan and refridgerate for at least 15 minutes
  9. Heat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit and bake scones for 20-25 minutes
  10. Serve with your choice of toppings
