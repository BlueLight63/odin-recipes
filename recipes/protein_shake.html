Protein Shake

Description

When you are feeling groggy in the morning and don't feel like putting any effort into breakfast, use this recipe to get you full and fueled up for the day with only 5 minutes of prep.

Ingredients

  • A blender bottle (any glass and spoon will also work)
  • Your favorite protein powder
  • 2 cups of milk
  1. Add protein powder to blender bottle
  2. Add milk to blender bottle
  3. Vigorously shake bottle until mixture is homogenous and there is no clumping
  4. Enjoy straight from the bottle
